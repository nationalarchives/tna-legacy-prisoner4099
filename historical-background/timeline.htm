Timeline

1861
William was born on 19th January at Kew Road, Richmond. His parents are William Robert and Mercy Towers (formerly Gridley).
1871
William appears on the 1871 census living at Bottons place in New Richmond, aged 10.
1872
On 4th January, William is sentenced to one month in Wandsworth prison.
1881

William appears on the new census, aged 20. He has moved with his parents to George Street, Battersea, about 10 miles from Richmond. William is a bricklayer, like his father and younger brother.

William marries, aged 21 Louisa Johnson, also 21. They are living at 44 Tugela Road, Battersea.

1891
On the 1891 census William was living at 26 Jews Row, Battersea and is married with four children. He also has his brother and a visitor staying with him.
1901
William appears on the census of 1901; he is living at 18 Flavell Road, Wandsworth with his wife and seven children
1906
William dies of locomotor ataxia on 24th January at Flavell Road, Battersea, aged 45.
